Vector Addition Simulation

Instructions:
1. Click "Add New Vector" to create vectors (maximum 5)
2. Drag vectors by their tails to move them around
3. Snap vector tails to vector tips to connect them
4. The resultant vector appears when all vectors are connected
5. Adjust scale so vectors fit on paper when you redraw them
Scale: 1 cm = 10 N
